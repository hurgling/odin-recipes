Lasagna

  • 1 pound sweet Italian Sausage
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1/2 Cup Minced Onion
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 16 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 and 1/2 lb of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Open up the cans of tomato sauce
  2. Pour them into the pot
  3. Throw the cheese and lasagna noodles in there
  4. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
