Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 8 large apples
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Storebought pie crust
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cloves
  • 1 tsp allspice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Roll out the pie crust into the bottom of the pan
  2. Chop apples roughly and add into pan
  3. Mix sugar with spices
  4. Shake dry ingredient mixture over apples
  5. Cut butter into 4 individual pats, and place evenly around pie
  6. Pour lemon juice over whole pie
  7. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes, until crust is browned and center gooey
